Dashiexp - Youtuber

DashieGames is the YouTube channel of Charlie Guzman, a Dominican-American Let’s Player who is known for his loud, hot-blooded commentary and excessive swearing while playing video games.. He has a large following on his gaming channel, where he plays a variety of games, including Mario Kart 64, Super Mario 64, Street Fighter games, Castlevania, fighting games, the Mega Man games, and Grand Theft Auto games. His videos are known for their comedy and entertainment value.

Gaming Channel - DashieGames

Guzman founded his most popular YouTube channel, DashieGames, on September 13, 2011, where he regularly posts video game commentary and streams. “DG - Dead Island Tease” is the first video on Charlie's DashieGames channel. In the video, which has over 415k views and over 17k likes as of August 2022, Charlie plays Dead Island as a teaser to kick off his channel as a throwback to his first ever video uploaded to his gaming channel.

Dashie's other channels - DashieXP & DashieXP2

He also has a channel secondary to this named DashieXP2, created on June 4, 2010, which features accompanying blooper reels to the skits as well as vlogs. However, he has since allocated the majority of his efforts to his gaming channel DashieGames.
